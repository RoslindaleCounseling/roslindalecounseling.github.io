Rates

Rachel’s goal is to provide excellent treatment and psychotherapy services for her clients. With this in mind she does not participate directly with managed care or insurance companies in order to respect her client’s confidentiality and independence. This allows Rachel and her clients to create treatment plans which best suit the individual not an insurance company. Her clients value the autonomy this provides them in setting goals and engaging in treatments tailored to their individual needs.

However, most insurance companies will reimburse you for a portion of your therapy costs. Upon request Rachel can provide you with a receipt of your payments which you can submit to your insurance company for out of plan provider reimbursement.

Rachel currently accepts cash or check only and payment is required at time of service.

If you need to miss a scheduled appointment please provide Rachel with at least 24 hours notice to avoid being charged for the session. Rachel does require credit card information to be kept on file in the case of a missed appointment and this card will be charged if a client does not provide 24 hours notice of cancellation. This policy is created out of respect for her clients, resources, and time. Thank you for your understanding.
