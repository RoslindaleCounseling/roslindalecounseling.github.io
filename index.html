Rachel believes in the power of the therapeutic relationship, CBT, EMDR, and Sensorimotor Psychotherapy techniques to help those who have experienced abuse, trauma, and suffer from depression and anxiety.

Rachel is currently practicing individual therapy with adults and adolescents in the Roslindale and Back Bay neighborhoods of Boston. In addition she is forming two support groups for those who have experienced sexual harassment or abuse in the work place (#UsToo) and a group for new parents.

Rachel is skilled in treating:
  • Anxiety (chronic worries, racing heart, tingling sensation in limbs, out of body experiences)
  • Depressed mood (hopelessness, sadness, feeling numb)
  • PTSD and survivors of abuse or those who have witnessed/experienced violence:
    • Always feeling “on edge”
    • Nightmares
    • Intrusive thoughts about the trauma
    • Avoiding anything that reminds you of the trauma
    • Difficulty regulating emotions
 If you are suffering from any of the above symptoms please contact Rachel today to learn how her services may help you.

There is hope! Anxiety, depression, and PTSD are all treatable conditions. You can find relief and healing now through the use of CBT, EMDR, and Sensorimotor Psychotherapy and a compassionate, therapeutic relationship with Rachel.
